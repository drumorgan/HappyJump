What is Happy Jump?

Happy Jump sells insured drug packages for completing a Happy Jump in Torn City. If you OD at any step, we cover the loss — no questions asked.

How It Works
1
Request a Package Click the purchase button below to submit a request. This doesn't cost anything yet — it just puts you in the queue. Your package includes:
4x Xanax
5x Erotic DVD
1x Ecstasy
2
In-Game Trade Giro Vagabondo will initiate a trade with you in Torn to deliver the items and collect payment. Once the trade is complete, your 7-day insurance window starts.
3
Use the items in order Take Xanax (4 times), watch all 5 EDVDs, then take Ecstasy. If everything goes clean — congrats, you've completed a Happy Jump!
4
OD? You're covered. If you OD at any step within the 7-day window, contact Giro. We'll send replacement items plus a cash rehab bonus. After 7 days with no OD, your deal closes clean — and that counts toward your loyalty tier.
